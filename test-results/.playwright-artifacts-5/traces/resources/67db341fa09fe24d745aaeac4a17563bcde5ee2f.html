Driver Safety Report For

Here is your Current Month Driver Safety Score:

High Risk Driver Low Risk Driver

Your Driver Safety Score is based on the following criteria:

  • Total miles driven
  • Miles driven at or above 80 mph
  • Hard or extreme breaking activity
  • Time spent driving very late at night

Last 30 days: Driver Safety Score Results

Miles Driven

*In general, the more miles you drive, the more likely you are to get into an accident.

Speed events at or above 80mph

*Accidents are more likely to occur and are more damaging at speeds at or above 80 mph.

Hard Braking

*Hard breaking occurs when a vehicle's speed decreases at a rate greater than 7 miles per second. Frequent hard brakes per day can indicate less cautious driving.

Hard Acceleration

*Hard acceleration occurs when a vehicle's speed increases at a rate greater than 7 miles per second. Frequent hard accelerations per day can indicate less cautious driving.

Time of Day

*Driving during the hours of midnight to 4 a.m. increases the likelihood that you will get into an accident.

Posted Speed Report

Instructions: Select your date range (maximum 30 days). Then select the Device Name and select the Posted Speed Limit (mph). This is the maximum mph allowed over the speed limit before it is considered a violation. E.g., if you choose 10, you will see a violation when your drivers go 10 mph over the posted speed limit. Finally, click Submit. The report will be queued up and may take up to 10 minutes to load. You can come back to this page to check the status.
